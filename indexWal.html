Brian worked as a Manager for Wal-Mart US between 2013 and 2015. He acted as Asset Protection Manager and Assistant Store Manager. Brian used de-escalation techniques learned from his time in the military on a daily basis to moderate customer interactions and provide service and solutions. He is well practiced in constructing reports and handling sensitive material.
As Asset Protection Manager he:
  • Managed shrink at store level through associate engagement, root cause analysis, process development and follow-up
  • Ensured store was compliant with federal and state safety and environmental regulations
  • Promoted a culture of a safety by encouraging, motivating, and developing associate involvement in accident trends within the store
  • Trained and managed a team of 6 Asset Protection Associates responsible for deterring theft as well as apprehending and referring suspects to law enforcement
  • Investigated sources of internal theft and created case files presented to law enforcement for prosecution
  • Participated monthly in audits of other facilities gauging the participation in company initiatives and standard operating procedures

As Assistant Store Manager he:
  • Performed as Operations, Front End, and Backroom Assistant Manager
  • Monitored store standards throughout the day by performing tours and assigning tasks to employees with specific expectations and deadlines
  • Was responsible for hiring, developing and coaching, and conducting terminations for supervisors and hourly employees
  • Analyzed monthly Profit & Loss statements for opportunities to increase sales and reduce costs
  • Developed store merchandising plans for October through February
  • Planned, coordinated, and executed the 2015 Black Friday Event resulting in over $400k in sales and no incidents as well as significantly reduced overtime costs compared to the previous year
