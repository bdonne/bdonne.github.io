Brian served in the U.S. Army Reserves from 2010 to 2014 as Military Police - MOS 31B as an E-4 Specialist. He deployed Active Duty from 2011 to 2012 to Guantanamo Bay, Cuba. In addition to military duties, Brian also led R&R events for soldiers during their deployment. On days off he captained a small boat in the Bay and led swimming excursions.
Duties performed:
  • Managed 12 personnel and their stations ensuring all deadlines for actions and reports were met
  • Relayed orders and changes to Standard Operating Procedures from leadership
  • Edited each stations reports, compiled statistics of activities using Excel each shift, and consolidated all statistics and reports of the day into a single brief
  • Trained new personnel in the Standard Operating Procedures for 6 different positions
  • Assisted in the refinement of the Training Manuals to reflect rapidly changing SOPs
  • Maintained and updated a training record of over 20 personnel
  • Administered and evaluated written and practical job qualification tests
